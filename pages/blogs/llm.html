LLM (Large Language Model)

Large Language Model

A Large Language Model (LLM) is a type of AI model designed to understand and generate human language. These models are trained on vast datasets comprising text from books, articles, websites, and other sources. LLMs, such as OpenAI's GPT-4 and Google's BERT, use transformer architectures to process and generate text. They can perform a wide range of tasks, including language translation, summarization, question answering, and content creation. The power of LLMs lies in their ability to understand context and generate human-like responses, making them useful in applications like chatbots, virtual assistants, and automated content generation.
