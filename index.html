Welcome To LVC!!!

A live virtual class is an online learning session conducted in real-time, where students and instructors interact through video conferencing tools. These classes offer the flexibility of attending from any location while maintaining the engagement and immediacy of a traditional classroom. Features often include live lectures, interactive discussions, screen sharing, and real-time feedback, making it a dynamic and effective way to learn.
